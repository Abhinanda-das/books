# LUNA Documentation

Welcome to LUNA Recording System! The LUNA Manual is your primary resource for setting up and operating LUNA.

The LUNA Manual is separated into three main areas of functionality: the LUNA application, UAD Instruments, and LUNA Extensions.

To navigate the LUNA Manual, enter the main areas below. You can use search to quickly find what you’re looking for.
