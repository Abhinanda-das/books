# Apollo Thunderbolt 2 Option Card System Requirements


## General Requirements

- Apollo Rack Audio Interface (Silver Apollo DUO and QUAD, Apollo 16, Apollo 8).
- Thunderbolt 2 cable (not included).\
  **Note**: Apollo X Gen 1 and 2 interfaces are not compatible the Thunderbolt 2 option card.
- Internet connection to download software and authorize UAD plug-ins.
- 25 gigabytes available storage.\
  **Note**: We highly recommend having at least 100 GB of free storage on your internal hard drive.

## macOS

- Available Thunderbolt 1, 2, 3, or 4 port.

<!-- -->

- <span style="font-weight: 400;">macOS Big Sur 11, Monterey 12, Ventura 13, Sonoma 14, Sequoia 15 and Tahoe 26</span>

<!-- -->

- **Note**: **UAD Software version 11.6.0** requires **macOS Big Sur 11** as a minimum operating system on Apple computers.
- Thunderbolt 3 connections require an Apple Thunderbolt 3 to Thunderbolt 2 Adapter (not included)

## Windows

- Available Thunderbolt 3 port\
  **Note**: Thunderbolt 2 devices are not compatible with Thunderbolt 4 on Windows
- Windows 10 64-bit edition or Windows 11
- Qualified Thunderbolt 3 to Thunderbolt adapter (not included)
