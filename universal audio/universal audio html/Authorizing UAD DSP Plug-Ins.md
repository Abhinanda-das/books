# Authorizing UAD DSP Plug-Ins


Please follow the instructions below to authorize your plug-ins from the **UAD Meter and Control Panel** application. To authorize your plug-ins offline, <a href="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/360051064611" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>read this article</strong></a>.

1.  Open the **UAD Meter & Control Panel** application.
2.  **Authorize Plug-Ins** in the **UAD Meter & Control Panel** application, by clicking the blue UAD-2 button. Under the **Plug-Ins** panel, click the **Authorize Plug-Ins** button.
3.  <span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">When the **Authorization Status** pop-up window appears, your plug-ins are ready for use. </span>\
    <span style="font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif;">Click **OK** to end the authorization process.</span>
