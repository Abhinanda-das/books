# Channel Strip Presets


![](Channel%20Strip%20Presets_assets/3e576ee7f1709a6960921e49be21b19b3b7fdf97.png)

*Channel strip presets in presets browser*

A Channel strip preset is a complete collection of plug-ins within a single channel, and all parameter settings within those plug-ins. Channel strip presets are saved as a disk file so they can be subsequently recalled into the same or a different channel. Channel strip preset files have the ".uadchannel" suffix.

Channel strip presets can be accessed and managed through the dedicated channel strip presets browser or on the Presets tab of the Plug-Ins & Presets browser.

- To open the channel strip preset browser, click the **+** next to the Inserts label on a mixer channel, or right click the channel label and choose Channel Presets.

![](Channel%20Strip%20Presets_assets/8f52b7cef20b597035c02b6878717ed3f4752a17.png)

- To manage presets from the Plug-Ins & Presets browser, click the Presets tab at the top of the Plug-Ins & Presets browser. If the Plug-Ins & Presets browser is not visible, choose View \> Section \> Browser from the UAD Console menus. 

![presets-in-plugs-presets-browser.png](Channel%20Strip%20Presets_assets/2dba6dee9c14821c322a77f157453141f734a2ed.png)

*Channel strip presets in Plug-Ins & Presets browser*

# Channel Strip Presets Folder

All channel strip presets reside in the Channel presets folder. 

**Note:** Channel strip presets can only be saved to the Channel strip presets folder.

## Channel Strip Presets Sub-Folders

The Channel Strip presets folder can contain one level of sub-folders for additional channel strip preset organization capability. The contents of sub-folders (if any) is displayed in the SUB-FOLDER column when a folder in the CHANNEL PRESET column is selected.

## Load, Save, Rename, Delete Channel Strip Presets

Channel Strip Preset files can be manipulated using the same methods as Plug-In Preset files. See [Using UAD Plug-In Presets](https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/25350711003924) for details.
