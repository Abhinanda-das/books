# LUNA Keyboard Shortcuts

The following shortcuts allow you to control LUNA with key commands. Note that you can <a href="35314657057172-Customizing-LUNA-Keyboard-Shortcuts.html" data-renderer-mark="true" title="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/35314657057172">customize</a> <a href="35314657057172-Customizing-LUNA-Keyboard-Shortcuts.html" data-renderer-mark="true" title="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/35314657057172">key commands</a>.

## Windows and Display

- **Toggle Timeline/Mixer**: Ctrl =

- **Next Window**: Ctrl \`

- **Previous Window**: Ctrl + Shift \`

- **Show/Hide Floating Windows**: Shift W

- **Close Application**: Ctrl W

- **New Alternate Window**: Shift + Ctrl =

- **Toggle Full Screen**: Ctrl + Shift =

# LUNA Menu Reference

The following menu items are available in LUNA. Note that not all menu items have key shortcuts. 

## LUNA menu

- **About LUNA**: Shows the LUNA version information.

- **Check for Updates...**: Checks the server for LUNA updates.

- **Preferences (Ctrl+,)**: Opens the LUNA settings page.

- **Hide LUNA (Ctrl+H)**: Hides LUNA windows.

- **Hide Others (Alt+Ctrl+H)**: Hides everything except LUNA windows.

- **Quit LUNA (Ctrl+Q)**: Quits LUNA.

 

## File menu

- **New (Ctrl+N)**: Open the LUNA New Session window.

- **Open... (Ctrl+O)**: Open a Finder window to open a session.

- **Open Recent**: Allows you to choose from a list of recent sessions to open.

- **Open Recent \> Browse All Recent (Alt+Ctrl+O)**: Opens the Recent Session browser.

- **Open Recent \> Clear Recent List**: Clears the Recent Sessions List.

- **Open Version (Shift+Ctrl+O)**: Open a session version, bookmark, or auto-backup.

- **Close (Ctrl+W)**: Closes the topmost floating window in the session.

- **Close Session (Shift+Ctrl+W)**: Closes the session.

- **Save Bookmark (Ctrl+S)**: Save the state of the session under the current version.

- **Save New Version (Shift+Ctrl+S)**: Save a new version within the session.

- **Duplicate…**: Saves a copy of the session with a new name.

- **Rename…**: Opens the Rename popover so you can rename the session.

- **Revert To**: Shows a list of automatically saved session bookmarks.

- **Import... (Ctrl+I)**: Opens a window to select audio or MIDI files to import.

- **Import Session… (Alt+I)**: Opens the Import Session Data window.

- **Export \> Mixdown (Alt+Ctrl+B)**: Opens the Export Mixdown dialog.

- **Export \> All Tracks as Files**: Opens the Export Mixdown dialog for all tracks.

- **Export \> Multitrack MIDI File**: Saves a multitrack MIDI file from the session.

- **Export \> Clips (Shift+Ctrl+K)**: Opens the Export Clips dialog.

 

## Edit menu

- **Undo (Ctrl+Z)**: Undo anything except a change made during recording.

- **Redo (Shift+Ctrl+Z)**: Redo anything except a change made during recording.

- **Cut (Ctrl+X)**: Cuts a selection to the clipboard.

- **Copy (Ctrl+C)**: Copies a selection to the clipboard.

- **Paste (Ctrl+V)**: Pastes a selection.

- **Clear (Delete)**: Deletes a selection.

- **Duplicate (Ctrl+D)**: Pastes a selection right after the current selection.

- **Consolidate (Alt+Shift+3)**: Combines two or more clips into one.

- **Reverse (Ctrl+Shift+R)**: Reverses a selection or clip.

- **Select All (Ctrl+A)**: Selects all clips on a timeline track.

- **Shift \> Cut (Shift+C)**: Cuts a selection and shifts clips to fill the space.

- **Shift \> Clear (Shift+Delete)**: Deletes selection and shifts clips to fill space.

- **Shift \> Paste (Shift+V)**: Pastes and shifts clips to accommodate data.

- **Shift \> Duplicate (Shift+D)**: Duplicates and shifts clips to accommodate data.

- **Shift \> Insert Time (Shift+I)**: Inserts space of the current selection length.

- **Nudge \> Left (,)**: Nudges the clip/selection to the left.

- **Nudge \> Left Next Amount (M)**: Nudges left by the next higher amount.

- **Nudge \> Right (.)**: Nudges the clip/selection to the right.

- **Nudge \> Right Next Amount (/)**: Nudges right by the next higher amount.

- **Nudge \> To Next Clip**: Nudges the selected clip to the boundary of the next clip.

- **Nudge \> To Previous Clip**: Nudges the clip to the boundary of the previous clip.

- **Nudge \> Increment Nudge Value (Alt+Ctrl+=)**: Increments the nudge amount.

- **Nudge \> Decrement Nudge Value (Alt+Ctrl+-)**: Decrements the nudge amount.

- **Nudge Content \> Left (Ctrl+,)**: Nudges content within the clip to the left.

- **Nudge Content \> Left Next Amount (Ctrl+M)**: Nudges content left by next higher amount.

- **Nudge Content \> Right (Ctrl+.)**: Nudges content within the clip to the right.

- **Nudge Content \> Right Next Amount (Ctrl+/)**: Nudges content right by next higher amount.

- **Mute Selection (Ctrl+M)**: Mutes a clip.

- **Separate Selection (Ctrl+E)**: Separates a clip at the playhead location.

- **Separate on Grid**: Separates selection into clips on every grid line.

- **Heal Separation (Ctrl+H)**: Recombines selected separated clips.

- **Create Fades (Ctrl+F)**: Creates a fade or crossfade.

- **Delete Fades**: Deletes fades in selected audio.

- **Fade to Clip Start (D)**: Fade in from playhead to start.

- **Fade to Clip Stop (G)**: Fade out from playhead to end.

- **Trim From Clip Start (A)**: Trims from the start of the clip to the playhead.

- **Trim From Clip End (S)**: Trims from the end of the clip to the playhead.

- **Trim Clip to Selection (Ctrl+T)**: Trims the clip to the selection.

- **Increase Grid (Shift+=)**: Increases grid setting by one increment.

- **Decrease Grid (Shift+-)**: Decreases grid setting by one increment.

- **Quantize (Ctrl+U)**: Quantizes notes based on current settings.

- **Quantize Settings (Shift+Ctrl+U)**: Opens the Quantize settings browser.

- **Set Bar One**: Sets the current playhead position as bar one.

- **Align Bar (Shift+Alt+Ctrl+I)**: Aligns playhead to bar and adds tempo event.

- **Align Grid (Alt+Ctrl+I)**: Aligns playhead to grid and adds tempo event.

 

## Track menu

- **New Tracks (Shift+Ctrl+N)**: Open the Create New Tracks dialog.

- **New Instrument (Shift+Ctrl+I)**: Creates a new instrument track with Shape.

- **New Track Group (Ctrl+G)**: Creates a track group with selected tracks.

- **Selection Grouping**: Enables or disables the Selection Grouping track group.

- **Suspend Groups (Shift+Ctrl+G)**: Turns all track groups on or off.

- **Duplicate (Alt+D)**: Duplicates selected tracks with content.

- **Duplicate Without Content (Shift+Alt+D)**: Duplicates tracks without content.

- **Bounce (Ctrl+B)**: Bounces an entire track or tracks.

- **Bounce Range (Alt+B)**: Bounces the selected range of a track.

- **Bounce Settings (Alt+Shift+B)**: Shows bounce settings.

- **New Version \> On All Tracks (Ctrl+Alt+)**: Creates a new Version on all tracks.

- **New Version \> On Selected Tracks (Ctrl+)**: Creates a new Version on selected tracks.

- **Duplicate Version \> On All Tracks (Ctrl+Alt+Ctrl+)**: Duplicates the selected Version on all tracks.

- **Duplicate Version \> On Selected Tracks (Ctrl+Alt+Shift+Ctrl+\|)**: Duplicates Version on selected tracks.

- **Select All Tracks (Shift+Ctrl+A)**: Selects all tracks.

- **Delete Selected Tracks (Shift+Ctrl+Delete)**: Deletes all selected tracks.

- **Hide Selected Tracks**: Hides all selected tracks.

- **Find Track (Alt+Ctrl+F)**: Opens Tracks Focus Browser search.

 

## Mixing menu

- **Solo Mode \> Latching**: Allows soloing multiple tracks.

- **Solo Mode \> Exclusive**: Only one element can be soloed at a time.

- **Create Bus (Shift+Ctrl+B)**: Creates a bus.

- **Assign Console Extension**: Opens Session Console Assignment browser.

- **Convert UAD-2 Plug-Ins to UADx**: Converts DSP plug-ins to native.

- **Convert UADx Plug-Ins to UAD-2**: Converts native plug-ins to DSP.

 

## Assistant menu

- **Assistant**: Enables the LUNA Assistant features.

- **Instrument Detection**: Toggles Instrument Detection on/off.

- **Instrument Detection Settings…**: Set options for colors and naming.

- **Run Instrument Detection**: Runs detection on current session.

- **Extract Tempo from Selection**: Extracts tempo from Timeline selection.

- **Extract Tempo from Entire Session**: Extracts tempo from entire session.

 

## Transport menu

- **Loop Playback (Ctrl+L)**: Enables looping for playback/record.

- **Play From Stop Location**: Stop button acts as Pause; playhead stays at stop location.

- **Pre-Roll**: Plays session for a period before the playhead/selection.

- **Post-Roll**: Plays session for a period after the selection ends.

- **Toggle Pre/Post-Roll (Ctrl+K)**: Toggles pre/post-roll on/off.

- **MIDI Merge (Numeric Keypad 9)**: Toggles MIDI Merge and Replace modes.

- **All Notes Off**: Stops all MIDI notes and messages.

- **Accelerated Realtime Monitoring**: Toggles ARM mode for low latency.

- **Console Tracking Mode**: Console inputs remain live/unmuted.

- **Toggle Playback (Spacebar)**: Starts or stops playback.

- **Toggle Record (Ctrl+Spacebar)**: Toggles recording.

- **Restart Playback (Ctrl+Alt+Ctrl+Spacebar)**: Restarts from original position.

- **Discard Recording (Shift+Ctrl+.)**: Stops and discards current recording.

- **Loop Ruler \> Update Loop (Ctrl+L)**: Matches Loop ruler to Timeline selection.

- **Loop Ruler \> Loop Select (Shift+Ctrl+L)**: Matches Timeline to Loop selection.

 

## Navigation menu

- **Zoom In (Ctrl+\])**: Zoom in horizontally.

- **Zoom Out (Ctrl+\[)**: Zoom out horizontally.

- **Frame Selection (E)**: Fills window with selection; press again to revert.

- **Return to Zero (Return)**: Moves playhead to session start.

- **Go to End**: Moves playhead to end of last clip.

- **Zoom In Audio (Alt+Ctrl+\])**: Zooms waveforms in horizontally.

- **Zoom Out Audio (Alt+Ctrl+\[)**: Zooms waveforms out horizontally.

- **Reset Audio Zoom**: Resets horizontal zoom for waveforms.

- **Selection \> Move Up (P)**: Moves selection to track above.

- **Selection \> Move Down (;)**: Moves selection to track below.

- **Selection \> Extend Up (Shift+P)**: Extends selection to track above.

- **Selection \> Extend Down (Shift+;)**: Extends selection to track below.

- **Selection \> Move to Next Transient (Tab)**: Moves playhead to next transient.

- **Selection \> Move to Previous Transient (Alt+Tab)**: Moves to previous transient.

- **Selection \> Move to Next Bar (\])**: Moves playhead to next bar.

- **Selection \> Move to Previous Bar (\[)**: Moves playhead to previous bar.

- **Track Heights \> Increase Selected (Ctrl+↑)**: Increases selected track height.

- **Track Heights \> Decrease Selected (Ctrl+↓)**: Decreases selected track height.

- **Auto-Scroll (Shift+A)**: Scrolls timeline automatically during playback.

 

## View menu

- **Section \> Browser**: Shows/hides the Focus Browser.

- **Section \> Focus Channel**: Shows Focus channel in Timeline.

- **Section \> Track Versions**: Shows Versions panel in track controls.

- **Section \> Monitor**: Shows Monitor section at the right.

- **MIDI Keyboard Mode (Alt+Ctrl+M)**: Toggles typing keyboard MIDI input.

- **Toggle Timeline/Mixer (Ctrl+=)**: Switches between Timeline/Mixer views.

 

## Windows menu

- **New Alternate Window (Shift+Ctrl+=)**: Opens a second LUNA window.

- **Previous Window (Shift+Ctrl+’)**: Cycles to the previous window.

- **Next Window (Ctrl+’)**: Cycles to the next window.

- **Show/Hide Floating Windows (Shift+W)**: Shows/hides floating windows.

 

## Help menu

- **Search**: Search for items in the menus.

- **LUNA Documentation**: Links to online documentation.

- **Give Feedback...**: Opens the Feedback form.

- **Show Keyboard Shortcuts**: Opens a list of keyboard shortcuts.

- **Tooltips**: Toggle between Off, On, and Help Mode.
