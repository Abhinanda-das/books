# FAQ: How to Use UAD Native Plug-Ins with NKS-Compatible Controllers


### What is NKS?

**NKS** stands for Native Kontrol Standard, a technology by Native Instruments that enables a seamless workflow between compatible hardware controllers and UAD Instrument and UAD effect plug-ins.

### What UAD Instruments and UAD plug-ins are compatible with NKS?

All UAD Instruments and UAD native plug-ins (UADx) are compatible with NKS. For full compatibility information, see <a href="41811941709076-NKS-Compatibility-with-UAD-Native-Plug-Ins.html" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">this article</a>. 

### What do I need before getting started using UAD plug-ins with a NKS-compatible controller? 

- NI Kontrol series keyboard, NI Maschine, or one of the following <a href="https://www.native-instruments.com/ni-tech-manuals/komplete-kontrol-manual/en/controller-integration#supported-midi-controllers" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">NKS-supported MIDI controllers</a>.
- NI Komplete Kontrol or NI Maschine software available from <a href="https://www.native-instruments.com/en/specials/native-access/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Native Access</span></a>.
- The latest updates for your UAD native plug-ins.
- The latest updates for your NKS hardware.

### How do I get started using NKS with UAD plug-ins?

1.  **Install Native Access -** Download and install <a href="https://www.native-instruments.com/en/specials/native-access/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Native Access</span></a>.
2.  **Log in to Native Access -** Log in to Native Access using your Native ID. If you do not have a Native ID yet, you can create one from within Native Access.
3.  **Install Komplete Kontrol or Maschine** - from the Library \> Applications tab within Native Access.

<!-- -->

4.  **Connect and power on your NKS-compatible controller** - Your controller may require you to load a specific template or preset for Komplete Kontrol, so be sure to check the documentation for your specific controller. 

#### **Using UAD Plug-Ins in Komplete Kontrol**

1.  **Open Komplete Kontrol -** Open either the standalone Komplete Kontrol app or the Komplete Kontrol plug-in in your preferred DAW.\
    \
    **Note:** If you are using Komplete Kontrol for the first time, you will need to open the standalone application to scan your plug-ins before they can be used in the Komplete Kontrol plug-in in your DAW.\
    \
    When launching the Komplete Kontrol standalone application, your plug-ins should be scanned automatically. If you need to manually rescan your plug-ins, click the **⋯… menu** in the header of Komplete Kontrol, select the **Edit** sub-menu, and then select **Preferences**. In the Preferences window that appears, click on the Plug-ins tab and then click the **Rescan** button.
2.  **Configure your controller** - Within Komplete Kontrol, click the **… menu** in the header. Then, in the **Controller** sub-menu, select **Controller Manager**, and double-click on your controller in the list. If you are using NI hardware, your controller will automatically be configured.\
    ![](FAQ%20How%20to%20Use%20UAD%20Native%20Plug-Ins%20with%20NKS-Compatible%20Controllers_assets/1576671445c71e282018967963ec7f67c9a4be5c.png)
3.  **Select an Instrument -** Use the **Instruments** menu to select an instrument. Once an instrument is selected, you can load audio effects plug-ins by clicking the **Plug-in Chain** button and selecting an empty slot in the chain. 

#### **Using UAD Plug-Ins in NI Maschine**

1.  **Open Maschine -** Open either the standalone Maschine app or the plug-in in your preferred DAW.
2.  **Select an Instrument or Effect -** Use the menus to select instruments or plug-ins, or find your sound in the preset browser.
