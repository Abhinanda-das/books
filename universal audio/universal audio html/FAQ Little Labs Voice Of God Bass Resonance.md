# FAQ: Little Labs Voice Of God Bass Resonance


Starting **April 2, 2026**, <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/little-labs-voice-of-god" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Little Labs Voice Of God Bass Resonance</a> is now available on the UAD Native platform. 

### I have yet to purchase <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/little-labs-voice-of-god" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Little Labs Voice Of God Bass Resonance</a> – do I get both UAD DSP and UAD Native licenses if I buy it?

Yes – purchasing this plug-in for the first time grants access to both UAD DSP and UAD Native licenses for **the limited introduction price of \$49**!

### I’m subscribed to UAD Spark. Is it included with my subscription?

Yes! Little Labs Voice Of God Bass Resonance is added to the roster of plug-ins included with all UAD Spark subscriptions. For a complete list of plug-ins included with UAD Spark subscriptions, read <a href="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/5230697410836" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">this article</a>.

### I already own the UAD DSP version. How can I get the UAD Native license?

Those who purchased the Voice Of God plug-in before the native version launched (April 2, 2026) are entitled to claim the UAD Native version for only **\$2.99** following the steps below:

1.  If you are entitled to this offer, navigate to the <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/little-labs-voice-of-god" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Little Labs Voice Of God Bass Resonance</a> product page and select the **Upgrade** option to add to your cart.\
    ![1_VoG_Upgrade.png](FAQ%20Little%20Labs%20Voice%20Of%20God%20Bass%20Resonance_assets/6474a1ba5d80768a40b13cc6486e1d944fa8f366.png)
2.  Click the **Add to Cart** button to add the **Upgrade** license to your cart, then go through the checkout process when you are ready to make the purchase.
3.  On the checkout page, the **\$2.99** price should be reflected to indicate you are purchasing the UAD Native license upgrade.
4.  Click the **Checkout** button at the bottom of the page to go through with the purchase and enter the requested payment information.
5.  When the purchase is completed, download your plug-in(s) via **UA Connect**. For more information about UA Connect and downloading UAD Native plug-ins, read <a href="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/5172589083028" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">this article</a>.

### How many UAD Native licenses can I authorize simultaneously?

If you purchase a perpetual license or UAD Native upgrade, it can be authorized on up to **three (3) locations**, including computers or iLok USBs.

For **UAD Spark** users, the license can be activated on up to **two (2) locations** – one to the iLok Cloud Session, the other to an iLok USB.
