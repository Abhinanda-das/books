# FAQ: Paradise Guitar Studio


![0_Paradise_hero.png](FAQ%20Paradise%20Guitar%20Studio_assets/2433eea24d2ade771bdbf160d243c31445e3db04.png)

<a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/paradise-guitar-studio" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>Paradise Guitar Studio</strong></a> is Universal Audio’s first end-to-end virtual studio for guitar. It gives guitar players and producers access to an entire high-end recording studio with authentic amp emulations, classic mic and speaker pairings, and onboard studio and stompbox effects inside a single, intuitive plug-in.

This article answers the most frequently asked questions regarding <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/paradise-guitar-studio" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>Paradise Guitar Studio</strong></a> and its associated offers.

#### How do I get Paradise Guitar Studio?

Paradise is available directly in the <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/collections/uad-plugins" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Store</strong></a> and from authorized UA retailers.

Alternatively, Paradise Guitar Studio is also included in all <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/uad-spark" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Spark</strong></a> subscriptions. 

#### Are there any Paradise discounts available for loyal customers?

You may find a discount if you’ve previously purchased the <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/uad-guitar-amp-bundle" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Guitar Amp Bundle</strong></a>.

To see if you qualify for a discount, you will need to add Paradise to your cart in the UAD Store and view the updated price at checkout. 

#### Is Paradise available in Custom Bundles?

No, Paradise is currently only available to purchase as an individual plug-in. Don’t forget that Paradise is also available in <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/uad-spark" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Spark</strong></a>.

#### Are there any plans to release any of the included effects as standalone plug-ins?

We do not currently have any news to announce about releasing any of the effects as plug-ins, but make sure to <a href="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/41256161691156-How-to-Submit-Feedback-to-Universal-Audio" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>leave feedback</strong></a>.
