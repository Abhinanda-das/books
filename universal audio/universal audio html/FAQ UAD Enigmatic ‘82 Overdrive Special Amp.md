# FAQ: UAD Enigmatic ‘82 Overdrive Special Amp


This article provides an overview of the <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/enigmatic-82-overdrive-special" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Enigmatic ‘82 Overdrive Special Amp plug-in</strong></a>, including its key features, system requirements, and how to get started.

#### What’s the UAD Enigmatic ‘82 Overdrive Special Amp?

The <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/enigmatic-82-overdrive-special" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Enigmatic ‘82 Overdrive Special Amp plug-in</strong></a> is a meticulously crafted software emulation of legendary and ultra-rare D-style amplifiers, widely considered the "Holy Grail" of boutique guitar tone. It meticulously recreates the circuit-level behavior of these fabled amps, giving you their famously dynamic cleans and singing overdrive.

#### I already own the UAFX Enigmatic '82 pedal. Is this the same thing?

The plug-in is based on the same acclaimed modeling technology found in the UAFX Enigmatic '82 pedal. The plug-in brings this exact tone to your DAW with flexibility for studio use, including the same deep customization options and premium, mix-ready cabinet and mic pairings.

Alternatively, UAD Enigmatic ‘82 is also included in all UAD Spark subscriptions.

#### Do I need Apollo or any UAD-2 hardware to use this plug-in?

No, <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/enigmatic-82-overdrive-special" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Enigmatic ‘82 Overdrive Special Amp</strong></a> is a UAD Native plug-in that runs natively on your computer, meaning you can use it in any compatible DAW with or without UAD or Apollo hardware.

Yes, if you get started with the <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/enigmatic-82-overdrive-special" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Enigmatic ‘82</strong></a> plug-in, you can later upgrade to the full <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/paradise-guitar-studio" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>Paradise Guitar Studio</strong></a> suite, which includes the <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/enigmatic-82-overdrive-special" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UAD Enigmatic ‘82</strong></a> amp plus a wider range of amps, effects, and advanced routing options for a complete guitar studio experience.

#### How does UAD Enigmatic ‘82 differ from the Enigmatic ‘82 amp in Paradise Guitar Studio?

UAD Enigmatic ‘82 is a standalone guitar amp plug-in that meticulously replicates the authentic ODS tone and is also available in Paradise Guitar Studio; the UAD Enigmatic ‘82 plug-in offers a standalone edition of these legendary guitar amps.
