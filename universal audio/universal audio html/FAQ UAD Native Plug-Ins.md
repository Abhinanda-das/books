# FAQ: UAD Native Plug-Ins


This article answers the most commonly asked questions regarding native plug-ins.

### iLok Questions

UAD Native plug-ins require an iLok account for activation. Please read below for common questions about iLok activations.

#### What do I need for iLok activation?

<a href="https://www.uaudio.com/downloads/ua-connect" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">UA Connect</a> will guide you through the process of creating a free iLok account or linking to an existing iLok account. **iLok Cloud** is used for **UAD Spark**, while **local host (computer) authorization** is used for **permanent licenses** – meaning no iLok USB is required. 

#### How many iLok activations do I get for each purchase?

Three activations are available for each UAD Nativex permanent license. **UAD Nativex plug-ins** and **LUNA licenses** can be activated to **local host (machine) locations** – on **up to three (3) locations** such as your **computers** or **iLok USBs**.

#### How many computers can I activate my UAD Native plug-ins on?* *

**Three (3) activations** are supported for permanent licenses of UAD Native products These activations can be authorized to a local host (i.e. a computer) and/or a physical iLok dongle. iLok Cloud only supports one computer connection at a time and is still used to authorize UAD Spark subscriptions.

#### Can I run UAD Native plug-ins offline?* *

Yes! You can now authorize your permanent plug-in licenses directly to your computer or to a physical iLok USB (2nd generation or higher) to authorize and run Native UAD plug-ins offline. 

**Note**: UAD Spark licenses can only be authorized to the **iLok Cloud** or to an **iLok USB**.

### Purchasing UAD Plugins

#### Where can I purchase UAD Native plug-ins?

Visit the [UA Store](https://www.uaudio.com/uad-plugins.html) or participating and authorized UA Dealers.

#### Can I try UAD Native plug-ins before I buy?

Yes, **14-day plug-in demos are available**. Find eligible plug-ins <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/uad-plugins.html?category_ids=.category_ids-12%2C.category_ids-70" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>on this page</strong></a>.

**Important Note:** Users can benefit only from one (1) demo period per plug-in. Demo resets are not possible for native plug-ins.

#### How do I get my UAD Native plug-ins?

After purchasing a plug-in, you will be guided to download and install <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/downloads/ua-connect" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>UA Connect</strong></a> to get your plug-in(s). To learn more about UA Connect, <a href="../sections/12733923748884.html" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>click here</strong></a>.

If you already have UA Connect installed, open the application to install your plug-in(s).

#### What about returns and refunds?

We do not offer returns or refunds for software.

### Native vs UAD DSP Questions

#### If I purchase a UAD plug-in, do I get a license for native and UAD DSP versions?

Yes – for every UAD plug-in that has both UAD Native and UAD DSP versions **available at the time of purchase**, you will get a license for both versions. To browse the collection of UAD Native plug-ins, visit <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/uad-plugins.html?category_ids=.category_ids-12%2C.category_ids-70" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>this page</strong></a>.

#### What are the advantages of using UAD DSP plug-ins versus UAD Native plug-ins?

- UAD DSP plug-ins allow you to record and monitor through UAD plug-ins with near-zero latency, regardless of your DAW buffer size.
- You get all the benefits of Unison technology.
- UAD DSP uses your UAD hardware’s DSP to process audio, easing the load on your computer’s CPU.

#### What are the advantages of using UAD Native plug-ins versus UAD DSP versions?

- You can mix on-the-go with UAD plug-ins without having UA hardware attached.
- UAD Native plug-ins allow you to run as many instances of a plug-in as your computer can handle.

#### Are UAD Native plug-ins priced the same as UAD DSP plug-ins?

Yes, for every UAD Plug-In that supports native and UAD DSP versions, your purchase includes a license for both. Whether you are buying for Native or Apollo (or both), one purchase is all you need. UAD DSP exclusive titles still require Apollo/UAD hardware to operate.

#### Do UAD Native plug-ins sound the same as UAD DSP plug-ins? 

Yes, native plug-ins sound identical to the UAD DSP plug-ins.

#### Do settings and/or presets translate from UAD DSP to UAD Native?

Yes, all plug-ins support copy/paste of settings between the two plug-in formats. With LUNA v1.4 (and higher), switching between native and DSP plug-in versions, along with their settings, can be done automatically.

#### Are all UAD plug-ins available natively?

Not all plug-ins are available natively. To find which plug-ins are available natively, visit <a href="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/5230697410836" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>this page</strong></a>.

#### Will there be more UAD plug-ins that run natively in the future? 

Yes, and they will be added to UAD Spark as they become available.

#### What’s the advantage of UAD Spark?

Subscribe for access to a growing collection of UAD Native plug-ins for one low price.

### Transfers

#### Will I be able to sell or transfer my UAD Native plug-ins to another UA account?

No, transfers of UAD Native plug-ins alone are not supported.

#### If I sell my Apollo, can I transfer the native versions of my UAD DSP license(s) to the new owner?

Yes. If you sell an Apollo and transfer all of your plug-ins to the new owner, the native versions are transferred along with the UAD DSP licenses. For more information about transferring hardware, <a href="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/206238366" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>read this article</strong></a>.

**Note**: Plug-ins cannot be transferred individually, or without transferring a DSP-enabled device such as **Apollo**, **Satellite** or **Arrow**.
