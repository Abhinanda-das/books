# How to Deactivate UAD Native (UADx) and LUNA licenses


This article demonstrates how to deactivate licenses for UAD Native (UADx) plug-ins, LUNA, and LUNA extensions.

1.  Open the iLok License Manager application - this should already be installed on your computer if you have UA Connect installed. Otherwise you can manually download and install it from [here](https://ilok.com/#!license-manager).
2.  Navigate to the "All Licenses," "Available," or "All Activations" tab.
3.  **Right-click** on the license and choose **Deactivate**.\
