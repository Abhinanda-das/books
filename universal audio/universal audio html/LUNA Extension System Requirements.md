# LUNA Extension System Requirements


This article lists all of the current system requirements for LUNA Extensions.

### **General Requirements**

- Requires the LUNA DAW (<span style="color: #191919;">LUNA system requirements are listed in </span><a href="360041532932-LUNA-System-Requirements.html" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">this article</span></strong></a>)
- <span style="color: #191919;">macOS Big Sur 11, Monterey 12, Ventura 13, Sonoma 14, Sequoia 15, and Tahoe 26</span>

<!-- -->

- <span style="color: #191919;">Windows 10 and newer \*</span>
- <span style="color: #191919;">Other required software:</span>
  - <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/pages/download-uad-apollo" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">UA Connect</span></strong></a><span style="color: #191919;"> – to install and authorize LUNA Extensions (UA Connect must remain installed) </span>
  - <span style="color: #191919;">**iLok License Manager** – included with UA Connect</span>
  - <span style="color: #191919;">Free </span><a href="https://www.ilok.com/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">PACE iLok</span></strong></a><span style="color: #191919;"> account – created for you when installing UA Connect</span>

<span style="color: #191919;">*\**</span> *Spitfire LUNA extensions are Mac-only and cannot be used on Windows*

### <span style="color: #434343;">**LUNA Spitfire Extension Requirements**</span>

- <span style="color: #191919;">Spitfire Instrument LUNA Extensions are Mac-only</span>
- <span style="color: #191919;">Requires SSD storage with APFS formatting</span>
- <span style="color: #191919;">1 GB to 10 GB of available SSD storage per plug-in (space requirements vary per UAD Instrument). **A minimum of 100 GB of available storage is highly recommended**.</span>
- <span style="color: #191919;">Individual Instrument storage requirements are listed on **UA Connect’s LUNA** tab.</span>

### <span style="color: #434343;">**Notes**</span>

- LUNA Extensions run exclusively inside LUNA and **cannot be used in other DAWs**.
- Most LUNA Extensions are compatible with the Mac and Windows versions of LUNA, except for the LUNA Spitfire Extensions listed below, which are Mac-only:
  - <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/luna-spitfire-bundle" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">LUNA Spitfire Bundle</span></strong></a>
  - <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/spitfire-chamber-strings-luna-edition" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Spitfire Chamber Strings</span></strong></a>
  - <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/spitfire-symphonic-brass-luna-edition" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Spitfire Symphonic Brass</span></strong></a>
  - <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/spitfire-symphonic-woodwinds-luna-edition" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Spitfire Symphonic Woodwinds</span></strong></a>

<!-- -->

- The <a href="https://www.uaudio.com/products/neve-summing" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong><span class="wysiwyg-underline">Neve Summing Extension</span></strong></a> requires a registered Apollo audio interface or UAD Accelerator to purchase. However, the extension itself runs natively once installed.
