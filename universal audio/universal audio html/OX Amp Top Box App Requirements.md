# OX Amp Top Box App Requirements


This brief article lists the system requirements for the OX Amp Top Box application.

- 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network with internet connection to register (once only)
- iPad: iPadOS 13 or later
- macOS: 10.15 Catalina or higher
- PC: Windows 10 or 11

**Note**: Tablets and 2-in-1 devices are not supported.
