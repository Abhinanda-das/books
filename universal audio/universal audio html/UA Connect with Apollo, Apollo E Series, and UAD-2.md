# UA Connect with Apollo, Apollo E Series, and UAD-2


Use UA Connect to register your Apollo and Apollo E Series hardware, and to download and install UAD DSP plug-ins, drivers, and UAD Console software. 

## Registering an Apollo or UAD-2 device

Go to the [hardware registration page](https://www.uaudio.com/my/account/register) to register your Apollo or UAD-2 hardware and get UA Connect.

## Registering and updating Apollo E Series devices

You can register an Apollo E Series device and update Apollo E Series device firmware (if available) within UA Connect.

### Registering an Apollo E Series device

1.  In UA Connect, click the Apollo E Series tab (under the Apollo item).
2.  Click Add Apollo Hardware.
3.  On the Set Up Your Hardware screen, choose the network port your Apollo E Series device is connected to, then click Scan For Devices.
4.  When your Apollo E Series device or devices are detected, select the device to register and click Add Device.\
    **Tip:** Click Identify next to a device to flash the power LED on the device.
5.  On the next screen, click Register.
6.  After the Apollo is registered, click Finish.

### Updating Apollo E Series firmware

After you register Apollo E Series hardware, the devices appear on the Apollo E Series screen in UA Connect. When a firmware update is available, the Update badge appears on the Apollo device. You can update firmware from UA Connect.

1.  Open UA Connect.
2.  Click the Apollo E Series tab (under the Apollo item).
3.  Select an Apollo E Series device.
4.  In the Firmware area, click Update, and follow the instructions to complete the firmware update.

## Installing UAD-2 Software with UA Connect

You install UAD-2 Software and UAD Console from UA Connect. 

1.  In UA Connect, under the Apollo item, click the Apollo & UAD-2 tab or the Apollo E Series tab. 
2.  Click the Download button next to UAD DSP Plug-Ins & Drivers. If UAD software is already installed, click the Update button.
3.  After the software is downloaded, click Install to complete the installation.

## Installing UAD Console with UA Connect

1.  In UA Connect, under the Apollo item, click the Apollo & UAD-2 tab or the Apollo E Series tab.
2.  Click the Download button next to UAD Console. If UAD Console is already installed, you can click the Update button (if an update is available).
3.  After the software is downloaded, click Install to complete the installation.

**Note:** UAD Console is automatically installed when you download UAD software.
