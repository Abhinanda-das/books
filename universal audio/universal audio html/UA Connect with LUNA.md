# UA Connect with LUNA


# Installing and Authorizing LUNA from UA Connect

To get LUNA, [download UA Connect](https://www.uaudio.com/downloads/luna).

1.  Install UA Connect.
2.  After installation, in UA Connect, click the LUNA tab.
3.  Click Get Started.\
    ![uac-new-luna.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20LUNA_assets/9a063d16de6461e07bacf5e956dae82cb60f1186.png)
4.  On the iLok setup screen, choose whether you have an iLok account, or whether to create a new iLok account. \
    ![ilok-setup-new.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20LUNA_assets/4911592057209dba8bc703245df8bf7f25d5132b.png)
5.  If you are creating a new iLok account, click Create Account. Keep note of the account name (the same as your email address). \
    If you are using an existing iLok account, type the account name and click Link Account. 
6.  On the LUNA page, click Redeem or Activate License next to the LUNA item, then Download LUNA. 
7.  Start LUNA from the Applications folder, macOS Dock, or Launchpad.
8.  When you are prompted to allow access to the Microphone, click OK.\
    **Note: You must allow LUNA to access the Microphone in order to record audio.**
9.  Complete the installation.

# About uaudio.com and iLok accounts

LUNA uses two different accounts.

- Your [uaudio.com](https://www.uaudio.com) account connects LUNA with the Universal Audio cloud, so you can purchase, download, and manage updates to LUNA, UAD instruments, and LUNA Extensions. You can also buy UAD plug-ins and access LUNA educational and informational resources with your [uaudio.com](https://www.uaudio.com) account.
- Your iLok account (iLok USB hardware, iLok local host, and/or iLok Cloud) authorizes the LUNA app, UAD instruments, and LUNA Extensions. You can either use an existing [iLok](https://www.ilok.com) account, or create one automatically and link it to LUNA during the installation process.

## Using iLok licenses

LUNA licenses can be used on two or three iLok USB (2nd or 3rd Generation) keys, or on two or three computers at a time. LUNA includes two or three authorizations for the LUNA app, and two licenses for each authorized Extension and Instrument. You can move iLok authorizations to physical iLok USB hardware keys to use your authorizations with multiple systems, or to your computer if you don't need a portable license.

**Note:** iLok Cloud licenses for LUNA and LUNA Extensions are being replaced with local host (machine) licenses. See [this article](35180558198548-FAQ-How-to-Activate-UAD-Plug-ins-and-Apps-on-Your-Computer.html) for more information. 

### Using an iLok USB or local host (computer) license

If your system is not continuously connected to the internet, or is only connected periodically, use a physical iLok key with a license installed, or authorize LUNA to your computer.

- You can install a LUNA license on your physical iLok hardware key by manually dragging and dropping the LUNA license into the key from within the iLok License Manager application.
- You can use a LUNA computer license by manually dragging and dropping the LUNA license onto your computer from within the iLok License Manager application.

On first run, LUNA may prompt you to choose a default license location (iLok Cloud, computer, or an iLok hardware key if you have one). Choose the license location that you prefer.

## Starting LUNA

1.  From Launchpad or the macOS Dock, or the Start menu in Windows, start LUNA.
2.  LUNA opens, and shows the session open/create page.

When you are logged in, lists of your installed products and other account details appear in the LUNA panel in UA Connect.
