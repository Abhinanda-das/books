# Managing Native UAD Plug-Ins with UA Connect 

Use UA Connect to:

- Activate your native UAD plug-ins.
- Download and install the latest versions of native UAD plug-ins to your computer. Native UAD plug-ins are installed in AAX, VST 3, and Audio Units (macOS only) formats.
- Remove installed native UAD plug-ins, and move UAD instrument content.
- Link an iLok User ID to your UA account (if one is not already linked) and switch plug-in licensing between ilok Cloud (UAD Spark only), iLok local host (machine), and iLok USB.\
  **Note:** UAD native (UADx) and LUNA licenses are issued as host computer licenses, and are licensed to your machine, even if you select iLok Cloud. Such licenses can also be [moved to an iLok USB with iLok License Manager](13406829288212-Using-UAD-Spark-Plug-In-Licenses-Offline.html).

## Getting started with UA Connect and native UAD plug-ins

1.  First, download and install UA Connect following the instructions in <a href="13178401374612-Getting-Started-with-UA-Connect.html" target="_self">this article</a>.
2.  When UA Connect is open, click the Access Spark & Native Plug-Ins button.

# Creating or Linking your iLok Account

Your native UAD plug-ins require an iLok account (an iLok User ID) to manage software licenses. If you have an iLok account, you can link it in this step. If you do not have an iLok account, a free account is created for you in this step. 

## iLok Notes 

- By default, UA Connect authorizes your perpetual plug-ins and LUNA software using iLok local host (machine) licenses. iLok local host licenses are provided for up to three machines, and can be moved between machines using iLok License Manager. See [this article](35180558198548-FAQ-How-to-Activate-UAD-Plug-ins-and-Apps-on-Your-Computer.html) for more information. 
- iLok Cloud licenses are available for UAD Spark subscriptions only. iLok Cloud does not require an iLok USB device, but does require a constant internet connection. If you do not have a constant connection, or want to store your licenses on an iLok USB for another reason, you can move your licenses to an iLok USB. See <a href="#h_01GS6FA2ZNCN88WQJTEMAPAY8V" target="_self">Managing the iLok license location</a> for details.
- If you have an iLok USB connected when you link your account, UA Connect prompts you to choose the license location. You can choose to license your native UAD plug-ins to your local machine or to an iLok USB.
- Most licenses can only be activated to a host computer location or an iLok USB, and cannot be licensed to iLok Cloud.

## When the iLok Setup screen appears:

If you have an iLok account, click I already Have an iLok Account, then enter your iLok User ID. If you do not have an iLok Account, click Create an iLok Account For Me, and the iLok account is created for you. The email address from your UA account is used to set up your iLok account.\
\
**Notes **

- **Your** iLok account is created without a password. If you need to log in to your iLok account in the future, go to ilok.com, click Sign In, and click the Forgot your User ID or password link.
- If you use LUNA, you already have an iLok User ID that is linked to your UA account. On the screen that prompts you to link your iLok account, your iLok User ID should already be populated. Click the Link Account button.

## Managing the iLok license location

You can set UA Connect to use iLok host (machine) or an iLok USB as the default location for native UAD plug-in licenses. 

**Note:** This setting does not move existing licenses between locations. To change the location of an already-installed license, use the iLok License Manager application. 

#### To set your iLok license location:

1.  In UA Connect, on the UAD Plug-Ins page or LUNA page, click Settings.
2.  Under License Location, click Manage.\
    ![settings-plugs-luna.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/698017c1ca325bc51b02e9b828391fcf2dcd10d7.png)
3.  Choose the license location for Perpetual Plug-Ins and for Spark Plug-Ins, and click Select. You can choose your local machine or an iLok USB for your perpetual plug-ins, and an iLok Cloud Session or an iLok USB for Spark plug-ins.

![license-location.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/cbf4c37907cc7370bd69a492df8913b67d20b84b.png)

# Getting your UAD Plug-Ins

After you have linked or created your iLok account, UA Connect opens the UAD Plug-Ins tab. Your UAD plug-ins appear in this area, and begin downloading automatically. You can download and use available native versions of any UAD plug-ins and UAD instruments that you own, if native versions are available.

## Redeeming plug-ins

In UA Connect, when a plug-in license is available in your account, the plug-in shows a button labeled "Redeem". To activate the license to your iLok account, click the Redeem button. The license is activated and the plug-in is downloaded and installed. 

## Activating plug-ins

Your native UAD plug-in licenses may be activated in iLok Cloud automatically. If your licenses are not yet activated (for example, with a demo or trial subscription), click on any Activate License button to activate your plug-ins. 

## If your UA account is not linked to plug-in licenses

If your account does not show any linked plug-in licenses, click Refresh in UA Connect to scan for any recent demos or purchase completed at uaudio.com.

If you want to try UAD plug-ins, or a UAD Spark subscription, visit [www.uaudio.com](https://www.uaudio.com/) to get a free 14-day demo for any native UAD plug-in, or to make a purchase.

# Buying Licenses for UAD Plug-Ins

You can buy permanent licenses for UAD plug-ins. For those plug-ins which have both native (UADx) and UAD-2 versions, you get licenses for both. 

**Note:** You can only buy individual licenses for Neve plug-ins if you own an Apollo. If you don’t own an Apollo, you can use native Neve plug-ins with a UAD Spark subscription. 

#### To buy UAD plug-in licenses from uaudio.com

1.  Visit the UA store at [www.uaudio.com](https://www.uaudio.com).
2.  Add plug-ins or plug-in bundles to your shopping cart. You’ll need to login to your UA account if not already logged in.
3.  When you are ready to complete your purchase, click the Shopping Cart icon at the top of the page, then click the CHECKOUT button. The payment page appears.
4.  Select the payment method and provide payment details. If applicable, apply any discount coupon codes.
5.  Click the PLACE YOUR ORDER button once only. The transaction is processed, the order confirmation page appears, and a confirmation email is sent. The UAD plug-in license(s) are issued to your UA account, and native UAD plug-in licenses for available plug-ins are added to your iLok account. 
6.  Open UA Connect. Click REFRESH. Your new plug-in(s) are displayed in the UAD Plug-Ins tab. 

#### To redeem UAD plug-in licenses from a partner site

1.  On the partner site, purchase the plug-in or plug-in bundle using the partner website and checkout. You will receive your license code via e-mail. 
2.  Visit uaudio.com/redeem, and log in to your account. 
3.  On the UA redeem page, use the license code you receive in the e-mail from the partner site. Your account is updated with the new plug-in(s).
4.  Open UA Connect. Click REFRESH.Your new plug-in(s) are displayed in the UAD Plug-Ins tab. 

To get the UAD-2 versions if you have an Apollo or UAD Accelerator, download the UAD Software [here](https://www.uaudio.com/downloads/uad).

# Installing Native UAD Plug-Ins

To download a native UAD plug-in, click its Download button to install it, or click the Download All button to download and install all plug-ins in one step. 

**Note:** Ravel Grand Piano does not download automatically (even when you click Download All) because it has specific storage requirements. Click Download next to Ravel to download Ravel. See <a href="#h_01GS6FN3N7XESR7YY692TJCSNB" rel="undefined" target="_self">Installing sample-based UAD instrument content</a> for more information.

## Installing sample-based UAD instrument content

When you start the download of a sample-based instrument that requires significant storage, such as Ravel Grand Piano, UA Connect prompts you with the storage requirements for the sample library. Choose the solid state (non-rotational) drive on which you want to install the sample library, then click Install.

![storage-requirements-macos.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/d7964833439888de22c49a5b2027c29d8724a7e4.png)

*Storage Requirements (macOS)*

![storage-requirements-win.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/00bf4333a0ef6a7106717edbd95b5e33147e360c.png)

*Storage Requirements (Windows)*

Ravel Grand Piano sample content must be installed on a solid state drive (SSD) that meets the following requirements:

- SSD drive with 10 GB available storage
- SSD must be formatted as APFS on macOS
- SSD must be formatted as NTFS on Windows
- If external, SSD must be inside a USB 3.0, USB 3.1, PCIe, or Thunderbolt enclosure

### Notes: 

- Spinning hard drives for sample-based instruments are unsupported on all platforms. macOS Fusion drives are unsupported on macOS.
- SSD drives formatted as ExFAT, FAT, and Mac OS Extended are unsupported.

To scan for drives after you add or remove a hard drive, click Rescan Drives.

If you click the Skip button after clicking Download All, all other titles to which you are entitled begin to download, but Ravel does not automatically download.

## Filtering plug-ins by license type

Select from the drop-down selector to filter plug-ins by the license type. The plug-in license categories are:

- **All Plug-Ins –** Shows all available plug-ins. In this view, plug-ins are labeled with Perpetual and/or UAD Spark labels to indicate the license type.
- **UAD Spark Plug-Ins –** Shows the plug-ins that are available as part of your UAD Spark subscription.
- **My Plug-Ins –** Shows the plug-ins for which you have a perpetual license.

## Updating a native UAD plug-in

When a newer native UAD plug-in is available, the Update button appears next to the plug-in. Click Update to install the latest version of the plug-in. Click Update All to update all plug-ins.

1.  To remove any native UAD plug-in, click the ••• menu to the right of the plug-in, and choose Uninstall.\
    ![move-menu-plugin.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/4b8322b987304235224a5c3f998474d542033e85.png)

## Installing sample content

For Ravel Grand Piano you can change the default storage location for the sample content (but not the plug-in). Because Ravel Grand Piano has large storage requirements, you can move its installed sample content to a different SSD drive.

### Changing the default content location

1.  In UA Connect, Click Settings.
2.  Under Instrument Content Location, click Change.\
    ![change-sample-location.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/df0f00bcb6ed8848952075f18d77649367231e51.png)
3.  In the Choose Content Drive window, choose the storage drive for the sample content and click Apply.\
    ![choose-content-drive.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/fc74feb5be6cd1278417ec77ea96805dd530f562.png)

To rescan your drives, if you have connected or removed a drive since you opened this window, click Rescan Drives.

**Note:** If you have already installed Ravel Grand Piano, the sample content is not moved. See Moving sample content to a different drive, following, to move content that is already installed. 

### Moving sample content to a different drive

1.  To move the sample content of an installed sample-based UADx instrument to a different drive, click the ••• menu to the right of the plug-in, and choose Move Content...\
    ![move-menu-plugin.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/4b8322b987304235224a5c3f998474d542033e85.png)\
     
2.  The Move Sample Content dialog appears.\
    ![move-content.png](UA%20Connect%20with%20Native%20UAD%20Plug-Ins_assets/0a81b62c29eafa81246caa6561f3cdec31a52c75.png)
3.  Choose a different drive for the sample-based UADx instrument, and click OK.

The plug-in sample content is moved to the new location.
