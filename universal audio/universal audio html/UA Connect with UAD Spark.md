# UA Connect with UAD Spark


Your UAD Spark is your subscription for native UAD plug-ins. For complete information on managing your native UAD plug-ins, <a href="5085235532052-UA-Connect-with-Native-UAD-Plug-Ins.html" target="_self">go here</a>.

# Setting up a UAD Spark Subscription with UA Connect

Start your UAD Spark subscription at [spark.uaudio.com](https://spark.uaudio.com/). If you haven’t already started your Spark subscription, UA Connect presents you with the option to visit [spark.uaudio.com](https://spark.uaudio.com/).

Follow these steps on the UAD Spark website to get started with your UAD Spark subscription and to download and install native UAD plug-ins. 

1.  Start your UAD Spark subscription at [spark.uaudio.com](https://spark.uaudio.com/). 
2.  Follow the link to download UA Connect from your e-mail, or download UA Connect at [**www.uaudio.com/downloads/ua-connect/**](https://www.uaudio.com/downloads/ua-connect/).
3.  In UA Connect, <a href="5085235532052-UA-Connect-with-Native-UAD-Plug-Ins.html#h_01GS6GDK6HBPNXX5TKKZTERFQP" target="_self">activate and install your native UAD plug-ins</a>.
