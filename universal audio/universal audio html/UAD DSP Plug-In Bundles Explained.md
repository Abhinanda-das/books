# UAD DSP Plug-In Bundles Explained


All newly registered Apollo Gen 1 devices purchased from an authorized retailer come with an included plug-in bundle free of charge. These bundles are a great way for new users to get started with UAD plug-ins right out of the box without the need for an additional purchase. The bundles included with each newly registered Apollo Gen 1 product are listed below including a list of the individual plug-ins included with each bundle. For answers to common questions regarding these plug-in bundles, please see the [FAQ at the bottom of this article](#FAQ).

**Note**: For information on bundled plug-ins with **Apollo Gen 2 devices**, <a href="https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/28530678475412" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank"><strong>read this article</strong></a>.

### <span style="font-weight: 400;">Frequently Asked Questions</span>

#### I already own some or all of the plug-ins in the included bundle. Can I replace the plug-ins I already own with alternate options?

No. The plug-ins are included with every UAD-2 device free of charge and we are unable to exchange these plug-ins for other choices. 

#### If I already own some or all of the plug-ins will I be issued duplicate licenses?

No. If you have been issued the plug-ins when registering another product you will not receive duplicate licenses. 

#### My computer uses an older operating system so I'm running an older version of UAD software. I don't see some of the Analog Classics plug-ins listed in my DAW. How do I use these plug-ins?

Some of the plug-ins contained in these bundles, such as the UA 610-B, Raw Distortion, and Precision Mix Rack Collection plug-ins were released in later versions of UAD software. If you are running an older UAD software version because of your computer's operating system, you may be unable to use some of these plug-ins without upgrading. The plug-in licenses will still be stored in your account however, so if you ever choose to update your operating system and UAD software, you will still be able to authorize those plug-ins at that point.

#### I own an Apollo interface and received the Marshall Plexi Classic plug-in as part of my Realtime Analog Classics bundle. Why can't I use the Marshall Plexi Super Lead 1959 plug-in?

The Marshall Plexi Classic plug-in that is included with Apollo interfaces is a light version of the full Marshall Plexi Super Lead 1959 plug-in. As an owner of the Marshall Plexi Classic plug-in, you are eligible to purchase the Marshall Plexi Super Lead 1959 plug-in from our web store at a discounted upgrade price.

#### I see the Precision Mix Rack Collection authorized in my account, but I don't see that plug-in listed in my DAW. How can I use the Precision Mix Rack Collection?

The Precision Mix Rack Collection is actually made up of four individual plug-ins: **Precision Channel Strip**, **Precision Reflection Engine**, **Precision Delay Mod**, and **Precision Delay Mod L**. For more information, read [this article](https://help.uaudio.com/hc/en-us/articles/209060646-Precision-Mix-Rack-Collection-Explained).
