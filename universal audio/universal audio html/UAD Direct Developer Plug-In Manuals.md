# UAD Direct Developer Plug-In Manuals


Some UAD DSP plug-in titles are created by our Direct Developer partners. Manuals for these 3rd-party plug-ins are written and provided by the developers.

This article contains links to all UAD DSP plug-in manuals provided by UA's Direct Developers, in alphabetical order.

**Notes**

- For plug-in manuals from Universal Audio (UAD DSP and UAD Native), [go here](../sections/4573072157460.html). 
- These manuals are in PDF format. PDF files require a free PDF reader application such as Preview (included with macOS), Edge (included with Windows), or <a href="https://get.adobe.com/reader/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Adobe Reader</a>.
