# Volt Specifications


### <span style="font-weight: 400;">Volt Specification Notes</span>

- Specifications apply to Volt desktop audio interface models (Volt 1, Volt 2, Volt 176, Volt 276, Volt 4, Volt 476, Volt 476P) except where noted. 
- Volt 876 specifications are in the [Volt 876 Hardware Manual](41288385879956-Volt-876-Hardware-Manual.html). Specifications are subject to change without notice.
- All Monitor, Headphone, and Line outputs are DC coupled. All inputs are AC coupled.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## System

### I/O Complement

**Mic/Line/Instrument Combo XLR/TRS Inputs**

- Volt 1: 1
- Volt 2: 2
- Volt 4: 2
- Volt 176: 1
- Volt 276: 2
- Volt 476: 2
- Volt 476P: 4

**Additional Line Inputs**

- Volt 4: 2
- Volt 476: 2
- Not available on Volt 1, 2, 176, 276, 476P

**Line Outputs**

- Volt 4: 4
- Volt 476: 4
- Volt 476P: 4
- Not available on Volt 1, 2, 176, 276

**Monitor Outputs**

- All models: 2

**Headphone Outputs**

- Volt 476P: 2
- All other models: 1

**Connectivity**

- USB: One USB 2.0 port (USB Type-C connector)
- MIDI: In/Out (two 5-pin DIN connectors)

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### A/D–D/A Conversion

- Supported sample rates: 44.1, 48, 88.2, 96, 176.4, 192 kHz
- Bit depth: 24 bit

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Analog I/O

### Mic, Line & Instrument Input Controls

**Vintage Mode**

- Available on: All Volt models
- Options: ON / OFF

**76 Compressor Presets**

- Available on: Volt 176, 276, 476
- Options: VOC, GTR, FAST, OFF
- Available on: Volt 476P
- Options: VOC, GTR, FAST (no OFF preset)

**76 Compressor Preset Timing**

- VOC: 2 ms attack, 300 ms release, 6:1 ratio
- GTR: 1.5 ms attack, 700 ms release, 6:1 ratio
- FAST: 30 µs attack, 35 ms release, 6:1 ratio

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Microphone Inputs

- Frequency response: 20 Hz – 20 kHz, ±0.1 dB
- Dynamic range (A-weighted): 112 dB
- THD+N (1 kHz @ –1 dBFS): –103 dB (0.0007%)
- Equivalent input noise (150 Ω, A-weighted): –127 dBu
- Maximum input level: 9 dBu
- Input impedance: 3 kΩ
- Available gain: 55 dB
- Phantom power: +48V (switchable)
- Connector: XLR Female, pin 2 positive

### Instrument Inputs

- Frequency response: 20 Hz – 20 kHz, ±0.05 dB
- Dynamic range (A-weighted): 111 dB
- THD+N (1 kHz @ –1 dBFS): –84 dB (0.006%)
- Maximum input level: 12.5 dBu
- Input impedance: 1 MΩ
- Gain range: 55 dB
- Connector: ¼" TS unbalanced

### Line Inputs (Inputs 1–2)

- Frequency response: 20 Hz – 20 kHz, ±0.05 dB
- Dynamic range (A-weighted): 111 dB
- THD+N (1 kHz @ –1 dBFS): –102 dB (0.0008%)
- Maximum input level: 22 dBu
- Input impedance: 15 kΩ
- Gain range: 55 dB
- Connector: ¼" TRS balanced or TS unbalanced

### Line Inputs 3–4 *(Volt 4 and 476 only)*

- Frequency response: 20 Hz – 20 kHz, ±0.05 dB
- Dynamic range (A-weighted): 113 dB
- THD+N (1 kHz @ –1 dBFS): –105 dB (0.0006%)
- Maximum input level: 18 dBu
- Input impedance: 10 kΩ
- Connector: ¼" TRS balanced or TS unbalanced

### Monitor & Line Outputs

- Frequency response: 20 Hz – 20 kHz, ±0.1 dB
- Dynamic range (A-weighted): 110 dB
- THD+N (1 kHz @ –1 dBFS): –102.5 dB (0.00075%)
- Maximum output level: 15 dBu
- Output impedance: 100 Ω
- Connector: ¼" TRS balanced or TS unbalanced

### Stereo Headphone Outputs

- Frequency response: 20 Hz – 20 kHz, ±0.1 dB
- Dynamic range (100 kΩ load, A-weighted): 110 dB
- THD+N (1 kHz @ –1 dBFS, 300 Ω): –100 dB (0.001%)
- Max output power into 32 Ω: 84 mW per channel
- Max output power into 300 Ω: 22 mW per channel
- Max output power into 600 Ω: 12 mW per channel
- Connector: ¼" TRS Stereo

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Electrical

- External DC power input: Barrel connector, 5.5mm OD / 2.1mm ID, IEC 60130-10 Type A
- Power supply requirements: +5VDC, Center Positive (+)

**Power Consumption**

- Volt 1: 2.8 W max, 1.9 W typical
- Volt 2: 4.2 W max, 2.5 W typical
- Volt 4: 6.2 W max, 4.3 W typical
- Volt 176: 3.2 W max, 2.3 W typical
- Volt 276: 5.0 W max, 3.5 W typical
- Volt 476: 6.7 W max, 4.9 W typical
- Volt 476P: 10.6 W max, 6.6 W typical

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Environmental

- Ambient temperature range: 32°–104°F (0°–40°C)
