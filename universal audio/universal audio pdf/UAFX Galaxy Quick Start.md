## **FEEDBACK** 

## **ECHO RATE** 

Delay time for active tape heads Clockwise for shorter echos 

## **HEAD SELECT** 

Cycles through tape head combinations, LEDs indicate active heads 

## **INPUT VOL [TAPE AGE]** 

Tape recording level **[ALT]** Tape wear, wow & flutter 

**ON LED** Lit when effects are active 

**ON SWITCH*** Toggles echo and reverb on/off

Echo repeats Self-oscillation at higher settings 

## **ECHO VOL** 

Tape echo level 100% wet at max 

## **[ALT] SELECT** 

Activates the [alternate] tape age, bass, and treble controls 

## **DWELL [BASS]** 

Drive into reverb tank **[ALT]** Echo bass, flat at noon 

**REVERB VOL [TREBLE]** Reverb level **[ALT]** Echo treble, flat at noon 

**TAP LED*** Blinks with tempo 

## **TAP | HOLD*** 

Sets echo tempo Hold for performance FX **Head 1** Dotted 16th note **Head 2** Dotted 8th note **Head 3** Quarter note
