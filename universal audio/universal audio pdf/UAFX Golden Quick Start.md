## **STORE** 

Hold down to save sound as preset 

**PREDELAY** Time before reverb onset 

**DECAY** Reverb time 

**EFFECT** Cycles through reverb types 

**BASS** 

**Spring 65, Plate 140** Low frequency response **Hall 224, Chamber & Plate 224** Low frequency decay 

**TREBLE** 

**Spring 65, Plate 140** High frequency response **Hall 224, Chamber & Plate 224** High frequency decay 

## **ON LED** 

Lit when knob settings active 

## **ON SWITCH** 

Toggle effect on/off with knob settings

## **Plate 140**
